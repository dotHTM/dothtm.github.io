An electric, flickering LED candle creates the appearance of characters at work inside.

![Door open showing the shelf in the upper position]({{ "/posts/art/image/tardis_up.jpeg" | absolute_url }})

As gifted to my friend, a tea-light sized LED battery powered candle is provided.

![Door open showing the shelf in the lower position]({{ "/posts/art/image/tardis_down.jpeg" | absolute_url }})

However the shelf can be stored aside if a taller light source is prefered.

Due to illness, the model was not delivered in early January as expected. With this additional time I could install a light seal inside the door, check for peeling fabric, as well as correcting warping due to inside not being sealed. With the warp no longer holding the door shut, a magnetic closure was installed.
