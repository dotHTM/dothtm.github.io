The box was originally requested by my wife, as a gift box for the Apple Watch inside. However, I quickly decided I wanted the box to be more functional beyond a mere conveyance of the gift, and to function as future storage of watch bands and accessories, or pieces of jewelry as desired.

<video controls>
<source src="{{ '/posts/art/video/watch_box_finished.mov.mp4' | absolute_url }}" alt="Finished box, demonstrated for function and internal dimension. Inside fits two Apple Swatch bodys, and several watch bands. More storage is available underneath.">
</video>

Fabrics are sourced from my passed mother's collection, stabilized with a 1:1 mix of regular Mod Podge and Elmer's Art Paste.

The project was completed in a single day from conception to final gluing, approximately 10 hours.
