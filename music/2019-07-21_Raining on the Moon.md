MIDI experiment where OP-1 Endless Sequencer drives both a rhythm section and the OP-Z lead synth, accompanied by programmed OP-Z percussion and base, and live played chords OP-Z.

Stereo to RCA splitter captures isolated instruments on left and right audio channels into Sony Recorder. Mastered in GarageBand for macOS.
